FAQ






Q: What does Nightlight Do ?

A: Nightlight uses Deep Learning Techniques to take pitch dark images and enhance the photo so that all the details are visible.



Q: What kind of images does nightlight support ?

A: Nightlight supports RAW images of .DNG (Adobe Digital Negative) format. RAW images are a special type of image that preserves all the details from the camera sensor. Other RAW formats used in cameras can easily be converted to .DNG using Adobe's official DNG Converter



Q: How to capture .DNG photos using a mobile phone ?

A: The official Adobe Lightroom app is capable of capturing using the DNG standard. They are available on both iOS and Android.
See the steps below on how to capture images using this app.

  1. Download the Adobe Lightroom app and signup for a free account.
  2. Click on the camera icon and capture image after ensuring that format is set to DNG.
  3. Go back to the main menu and Go to All Photos.
  4. Go to desired photo and select the menu on the top right and press export original

Q: What kind of cameras does nightlight support ?

A: Nightlight will work well with any good DSLR camera that supports shooting in RAW mode. In case of phone cameras it supports any phone with a decently good camera preferably with a Sony IMX series or Samsung Isocell GM Series sensor.
